Effects of Alcohol

Drinking in your teens is dumb enough to have a lot of adverse effects such as higher chances of accidents, depression or getting on the wrong side with cops. It also creates problems in decision-making, which is a part of the reason for long-term alcohol-related issues. Alcohol misuse can affect the way teens think and act, leading them to make unsafe decisions. A delay in the introduction of alcohol consumption to adulthood contributes to a reduction of these value. Individually, also the parents viewpoint concerning alcohol performs a radical part in determining on what their kids will decide.

Overcoming Alcohol

If you are struggling with alcohol or other issues, Youthline and the Nana Muru Youth Program can assist. Youthline is a helpline that you can reach either by phone, call or email where they help to provide counsel and connect your problems with the appropriate agencies plus helps in quitting drinking. Likewise, the Nana Muru Youth Program offers a range of free support including individual counseling, group sessions and life skills education. These are programs that offer teens working tools, exercises for awareness and motivate systemic change.
